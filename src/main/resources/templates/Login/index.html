Welcome to Appeaz, your job or school application tracker!

Register or Login to organize your job or school search. Appeaz allows you to keep all of your applications in one, secure place with access to the actual job application with one click.

Features include:

  • Store crucial information (benefits, salary, location, etc.) about each application
  • Utilize our "Peaz Rating" system to compare applications by various factors
  • Easily sort applications to allow for further organization
  • Quickly add, delete, or edit applications
  • Add your address to determine commute times
  • And many more!
