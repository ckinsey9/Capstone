Welcome to Appeaz, your job or school application tracker!

Register or Login to organize your job or school search. Appeaz allows you to keep all your application in one, secure place with access to the actual job application with one click.

Features include:

  • Store crucial information (benefits, salary, location, etc.) about each application
  • Easily sort applications to allow for further organization
  • Quickly add, delete, or edit applications
  • Add your address to determine commute times
  • And many more!
